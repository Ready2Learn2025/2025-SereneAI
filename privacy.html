1. Who we are and how to contact us

SereneAI Ltd ("SereneAI", "we", "us") is a company registered in England and Wales. We specialise in AI consulting for UK organisations. Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages or Netlify. If you have questions about this policy, please email info@sereneai.co.uk or write to our registered office (address available on request).

2. What personal data we collect and why

  • Contact forms. When you contact us through our Netlify forms, we collect your name, email address and message so we can respond.
  • User accounts. We may introduce login functionality using Firebase in future. If you create an account, we will collect your email address and other necessary details.
  • Prompt submissions. If you submit prompts to our public Prompt Library, we record the text of your prompt along with the name and LinkedIn profile link you provide. This helps us credit your work and manage the library.

3. Legal basis for processing data

We process personal data under the following UK GDPR bases:

  • Consent – when you provide information via forms or create an account.
  • Legitimate interests – to operate and improve our services, including managing the Prompt Library.
  • Legal obligation – to comply with UK law.

4. How data is stored and secured

Your form submissions are stored by Netlify. Any user account details will be stored in Firebase. We restrict access to authorised personnel and use appropriate technical measures such as encryption in transit. Data is stored in the UK or EEA where possible.

5. Third-party services used

  • Netlify – hosts our site and processes contact form submissions.
  • GitHub Pages – may host static content.
  • Firebase – may provide authentication and database features for future services.

Each provider has its own privacy policy and may process data outside the UK. We ensure that adequate safeguards are in place.

6. Cookies

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Only functional cookies necessary for site operation or minimal analytics may be set by our service providers.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have rights to access, rectify, erase and restrict the processing of your personal data. You may also object to processing and request data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

8. How to make a complaint

If you have concerns about our use of your data, please contact us first so we can resolve the issue. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you are not satisfied with our response.

10. Prompt Library

When you submit a prompt to our Prompt Library you confirm that you are the original creator. By submitting, you grant SereneAI Ltd and the public a free, irrevocable licence to use, reproduce and share the prompt for any purpose, including commercial uses. We publish prompts with credit using the name and optional LinkedIn profile link you provide. SereneAI may moderate, edit or remove submissions at our discretion.
